What's a Static Website?

A static website features fixed content that doesn't change unless manually updated by a developer. It's a simple, information-focused site that provides the same content to all users. Unlike dynamic websites, it doesn't generate content on the spot or support real-time interactions.

What's a E-Commerce Website?

An e-commerce website is an online platform where businesses showcase and sell products or services, and customers can browse, select items, and make secure purchases over the internet. It offers a convenient and efficient way to shop and conduct transactions online.
